This picture was taken in Coron, Palawan, a stunning tropical destination in the Philippines, located in the northern part of Palawan province on and around Busuanga Island and nearby islets. It is renowned for its dramatic natural scenery, clear blue waters, and rich marine life — a paradise for nature lovers, beachgoers, snorkelers, and divers alike.

The Basilica Minore del Santo Niño de Cebú—often simply called Sto. Niño Church or Santo Niño Basilica—is a Roman Catholic basilica in Cebu City, Philippines. It’s widely recognized as the oldest church in the country, dating back to 1565, and remains an active place of worship and pilgrimage.
